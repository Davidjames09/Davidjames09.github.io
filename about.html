About Me

David D. James is a distinguished veteran and accomplished professional whose career encompasses significant healthcare, leadership, and technology achievements. With foundational training from the Medical Education & Training Campus and an Associate's degree in Health Science from Kaplan University, David has honed a multidisciplinary skill set. His dedication to excellence is evidenced by his receipt of multiple Army Achievement Medals and Good Conduct Medals, acknowledging his integrity, leadership, and commitment.

In his civilian career, David has transitioned his military discipline into pharmacy management and IT, showcasing versatility and a commitment to service. His roles at Kennesaw State University as a Student Assistant Technical Support Analyst, his contributions to the technology sector through positions at Network Allies, LLC, and an internship at KPMG US reflect his adaptability and drive for continuous learning and improvement.

David's academic pursuits are matched by his commitment to professional development, demonstrated through specialized courses in pharmaceutical waste disposal and HR management. His journey from a dedicated serviceman to a tech-savvy professional and educator is a testament to his relentless pursuit of growth and excellence. David D. James embodies the principles of leadership, innovation, and service, making significant contributions to every field he touches.
